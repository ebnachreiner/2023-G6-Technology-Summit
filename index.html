Our Code of Conduct

Harassment

We will not tolerate any form of discrimination or harassment, which includes offensive verbal comments involving race, ethnicity, gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, appearance and body size, technical experience, or religion.
Other behaviors prohibited by our anti-harassment policy include (but are not limited to): stalking, intimidation, unwelcome photography, unwelcome sexual advances, and the deliberate disruption of a speaker.

User-Facing Procedures

Reporting Harassment or inappropriate behavior: If you experience or witness harassment or inappropriate behavior at the event, please report it immediately to one of the event organizers or staff members. We encourage doing this in person for a more immediate response, however, you can also do this by emailing us at example_email@gmail.com. The staff will keep all reports confidential.

Possible actions: Depending on the severity of the behavior, the event organizers may take many actions, including but not limited to the following:

  • Warning the person to stop the behavior
  • Requiring the person to leave the event
  • Banning the person from attending future events
  • Involving law enforcement, if necessary

Inclusivity

We are dedicated to creating a safe and inclusive environment for everyone, an environment where all our members feel respected, valued and empowered regardless of their age, race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic.
Behavior and comments that intend to be discriminatory and offensive will not be tolerated and will be subject to our procedural guidelines.

User-Facing Procedures

If a member feels that they have been subjected to discrimination or offensive behavior, they should report it immediately to a staff member or through our reporting system (see above).
If you require special accommodations to fully participate in our programs or events, please contact our board in advance. We will make every effort to provide reasonable accommodations to meet your needs.

Possible actions: We reserve the right to take appropriate disciplinary action, including suspension or termination of membership, in cases where a member violates our inclusivity policies.

  • Warning the member to stop the behavior
  • Suspending or terminating the member's membership
  • Involving law enforcement, if necessary
  • Investigate discriminatory and/or offensive behavior

Physical Safety

All attendees and members of the event must respect each others’ physical boundaries. Possession of dangerous or unauthorized materials such as explosives, firearms, weapons or similar items are prohibited in this event.
We will respect the privacy and confidentiality of anyone who reports an incident of physical harassment or assault.

User-Facing Procedures

Reporting suspicious activities

  • “If You See Something, Say Something”
  • If you see something, please report it immediately to any staff member or event organizer. We encourage doing this to ensure everyone’s safety.
  • Provide a safe and private reporting system for anyone who experiences or witnesses physical harassment or assault
Possible Actions: Depending on the severity, event organizers may first assess the situation to determine the level of danger or harm involved. If someone is in immediate danger, do not hesitate to contact emergency and/or security personnel.
  • Revoke the violator’s membership
  • Suspend the violator’s attendance at future events
  • Designate staff members present to monitor and respond to any incidents of physical harassment or assault.
Individuals involved in physical altercations may be asked to leave the event immediately. The same applies to possession of firearms, explosives, weapons or similar items.
